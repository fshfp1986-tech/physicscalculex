About Physics Calculex

Physics Calculex is a simple educational physics calculator app designed for quick calculations. It helps users calculate commonly used physics values such as velocity, force, energy, pressure, density, momentum, wave speed, frequency, Ohm's law and resistance.

Privacy Policy

Physics Calculex does not require login, does not collect personal information, does not access contacts, location, camera, microphone, SMS, files or sensitive device data. Calculations are performed inside the app/browser on the user's device.

If ads, analytics or third-party SDKs are added in the future, this privacy policy and Google Play Data Safety section must be updated accordingly.

Educational Disclaimer

This app is made for educational and learning purposes. Results are based on standard physics formulas. Users should verify important academic, laboratory or engineering calculations with a teacher, professional or trusted reference.
